Our Story

Ecuador changed our lives.

Years ago, what began as a journey became a lifelong commitment. We found ourselves welcomed into communities that viewed plants not as products, but as living teachers.

Those relationships continue to shape every formulation, every retreat, and every story we share. Today we live in the cloud forest, nestled between the Andes and the Amazon, an hour and a half from the communities who taught us everything.
