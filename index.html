Our Philosophy

Kawsaypac means "for life." Every pouch begins in the wild, with the healers who know these plants best, and ends in your daily ritual.

"The land teaches. The plants heal. We are only here to listen."

Our Story

Ecuador changed our lives

We are Raisa and Brian. What started as a move to Ecuador became a calling: the plants that have kept families here well for generations deserve to be honored, not forgotten.

We work directly with Indigenous harvesters in the Andes and the Amazon, pay fairly, and take only what the land can give. Every pouch is a bridge between their wisdom and your kitchen.
