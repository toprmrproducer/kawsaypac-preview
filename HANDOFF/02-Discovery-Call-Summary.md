# Discovery Call Summary — Raisa (The Electric Eats)
*27-min call, 27 Jun 2026. Full verbatim transcript in 01-Discovery-Call-Transcript.txt.*

## Who + what
- Raisa + husband Brian run **The Electric Eats / Kawsaypac** solo. Wildcrafted Ecuadorian herbs (raw pouches, not tea bags) from the Andes/Amazon, sourced directly from indigenous communities, sit with healers/shamans. Plus digital eBooks and high-ticket in-person retreats in Ecuador.
- Been running 2 years (Shopify 4 years, herbs the last 2). Current theme ~1.5 years old, no real facelift since.

## The differentiator
Direct-from-the-jungle sourcing. Nobody else goes into the Amazon and shows where herbs come from or how healers have used them for centuries. Competitors import from Jamaica or buy secondary market. This authenticity is the whole brand.

## Numbers (from the call)
- Channels: 100% organic. Instagram (biggest) + Facebook (secondary), dormant TikTok. Never run meta ads. Funnels via DM + link → Shopify checkout.
- Orders: ~200 good month, ~70 slow month. AOV $35-55, hundreds if international.
- Conversion: recently ~2% (was higher, dipped after a move; ballpark ~10% historically).
- Revenue via site: only **$600-800/mo** right now (energy went to an August retreat).
- CAC: effectively zero (organic, live near the jungle).

## ICP
75% women, 25-50, core 30-40. BUT a meaningful slice are **45-55 and struggle with websites**. Senior-friendly UX is a HARD requirement.

## Goal + motivation
- **$30,000/month minimum within 12 months** (herbs + digital + retreats; retreats are the high-ticket driver).
- Motivation: consistency after moving 1.5h from the jungle → then Facebook ads → then starting a family. Commitment "20 out of 10."

## Pain / why us
- Site "looks like any average Joe website." Trust built in DMs, not on-site. She has no time to build it herself (only real blocker) + a learning curve. Wants a polished, standout, mobile-first site before running ads and before Black Friday.

## What was sold
- $1,500 one-time: full custom conversion-focused front end, Shopify stays backend (headless), a month of SEO, AI chatbot for a month, product creatives + AI review videos, done-for-you start to finish, ~1 week (2 weeks worst case), 12-24h update cadence.
- 50% ($750) upfront, 50% on completion. Pays by card. Prefers WhatsApp over email.
