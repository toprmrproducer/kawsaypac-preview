Our story

Ecuador changed our lives

We are Raisa and Brian. What began as a search for answers became a return to the land, the plants, and the people who remember.

The path so far

A journey back to what was never lost

Beginning in 2022, we traveled through the Andes and Amazon as students. In 2025, we made the Ecuadorian cloud forest home. Kawsaypac grew from the relationships, teachings, and responsibility that followed.
